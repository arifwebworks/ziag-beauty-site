Frequently Asked Questions

What makes ZiaG Beauty Soap different from other soaps?

ZiaG Beauty Soap is made with 100% natural ingredients, handcrafted with care in small batches to ensure quality. Unlike commercial soaps that often contain harsh chemicals, our soap is gentle on the skin, providing multiple benefits including skin brightening, moisturizing, and anti-aging properties.

How long does one soap bar last?

The duration depends on usage frequency and how well you care for the soap. With proper use and storing the soap in a well-draining soap dish when not in use, one bar can last approximately 3-4 weeks with daily use.

Is this soap suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes, our Rose Edition Beauty Soap is formulated to be gentle and suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin. However, if you have specific skin conditions or allergies, we recommend patch testing before regular use.

How should I store the soap to make it last longer?

Store your soap in a well-draining soap dish in a cool, dry place. This allows the soap to dry between uses, preventing it from dissolving too quickly. Avoid leaving it in direct water or a wet environment.
